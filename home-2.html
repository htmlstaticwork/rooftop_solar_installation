Common Questions

Does my roof need to be new?

Not necessarily, but we recommend your roof has at least 10 years of life left. We provide a full assessment before installation.

How long does the installation take?

The physical installation usually takes 1 to 3 days depending on the size of your system.

What happens on cloudy days?

Your solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days, just at a lower output. Your battery backup or the grid will provide the rest.
