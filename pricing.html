Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our solar pricing and installation process.

How much can I actually save?

Most of our customers eliminate 90-100% of their monthly electricity costs. The exact savings depend on your roof orientation, local utility rates, and household energy consumption patterns.

What happens during cloudy days?

Your system still generates power during overcast weather, just at a reduced rate. Our high-efficiency monocrystalline panels are specifically designed to capture diffuse sunlight effectively.

Do you provide financing options?

Yes! We offer $0-down solar leases, low-interest loans, and performance-based financing. Our team will help you find the most cost-effective option for your specific financial situation.

Is there a warranty on the panels?

Absolutely. All our premium plans come with a 25-year linear performance warranty and a 10-year comprehensive workmanship guarantee for complete long-term peace of mind.

How long does installation take?

Once all structural permits are approved by your local authority, the actual on-roof installation typically takes just 1-2 business days for standard residential properties.

Does solar increase home value?

Yes, multiple real estate studies show that homes with solar installations sell for an average of 4.1% more than homes without them, and often sell faster in the competitive market.
