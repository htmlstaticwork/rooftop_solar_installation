Energy Independence with Smart Storage

Solar batteries store excess energy generated by your panels during the day, so you can use it at night or during power outages. This maximizes your self-consumption and provides total peace of mind.

Our lithium-ion storage solutions are sleek, wall-mounted, and fully integrated with your solar inverter, allowing for seamless transitions between solar, battery, and grid power without even a flicker of your lights.

How We Integrate Your Battery

1

Load Profiling

2

3

App Synchronization

Connecting your battery to the cloud for 24/7 monitoring and performance optimization.
